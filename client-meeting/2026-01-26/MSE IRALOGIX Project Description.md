## Business Motivation

The rapid expansion of state-mandated retirement programs—active in 20 states and covering over 40 million workers—has created regulatory pressure and operational complexity for providers. Employers, particularly small businesses, face significant penalties for non-compliance, such as fines in California ranging from $250 to $500 per employee.

IRALOGIX aims to address this by creating a rules engine backed by jurisdictional-specific knowledge. The MSE team will develop a proof-of-concept to:

- Ingest jurisdictional requirements.
- Evaluate employer characteristics.
- Generate compliance recommendations.
- Turn regulatory burdens into a frictionless "onramp" for retirement participation.

---

## Project Description

The system consists of five core components:

1. **Jurisdiction Intelligence Engine:** A knowledge graph maintaining real-time regulatory rules across state programs.
2. **Eligibility Determination Service:** A multi-criteria decision system for complex employer scenarios.
3. **Compliance Deadline Calculator:** Sophisticated arithmetic for phase-in schedules.
4. **Automated Onboarding Workflow:** A process designed to reduce onboarding time from days to hours.
5. **Monitoring & Alert System:** Proactive tracking with graduated notifications.
